🍛 Main Dishes

Chicken Adobo

⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5)
⏱️ 15 Mins | 👥 4 Servings

Ingredients:

Steps:

  1. Sauté garlic in oil.
  2. Add chicken and brown both sides.
  3. Pour vinegar and soy sauce.
  4. Simmer 20 minutes.
  5. Serve hot.

Tinolang Manok

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5.0)
⏱️ 15 Mins | 👥 4 Servings

Ingredients:

Steps:

  1. Sauté garlic, onion, and ginger.
  2. Add chicken and cook lightly.
  3. Pour water and fish sauce.
  4. Add papaya and simmer until tender.
  5. Add leaves and serve.

Leche Flan

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5.0)
⏱️ 40 Mins | 👥 2 Servings

Ingredients:

Steps:

  1. Caramelize sugar in molds.
  2. Mix egg yolks, condensed & evaporated milk, and vanilla.
  3. Pour mixture into molds.
  4. Steam 30-40 mins until set.
  5. Cool, refrigerate, and serve.
